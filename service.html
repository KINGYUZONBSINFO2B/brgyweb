Our Services

Barangay Banago offers a range of essential services to support and empower our community. Discover the programs and resources we provide to ensure the well-being and growth of our residents.

Health and Wellness Programs

We prioritize the health of our citizens by offering regular check-ups, free immunizations, and wellness workshops that address various health topics, such as proper nutrition, mental health, and preventive care.

Community Clean-Up and Environmental Initiatives

Our community actively engages in regular clean-up drives and tree-planting events to preserve and improve our environment. We aim to create a sustainable and eco-friendly barangay for future generations.
